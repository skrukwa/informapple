Welcome to Informapple!

An analytical project which scrapes top Apple news articles and compares the sentiment of those articles to Apple stock price over time.

Developed by Evan Skrukwa and powered by Python.

Crowdsource top 25 Apple news articles every month (3 articles in example below).

Scrape and filter article content using libraries such as scrapy, Newspaper, and readability (news-please).

Analyze sentiment using target-dependent sentiment (NewsMTSC) and natural language processing (FlairNLP).

Calculate average sentiment for the month and add to results plot.
